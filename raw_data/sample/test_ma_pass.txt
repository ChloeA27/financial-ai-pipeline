Advanced Micro Devices, Inc.
Acquisition of Silo AI

SANTA CLARA, Calif. – January 15, 2026 – AMD (NASDAQ: AMD) today
announced a definitive agreement to acquire Silo AI, a leading provider
of enterprise AI inference solutions, in an all-cash transaction valued
at approximately $4.9 billion.

Under the terms of the agreement, AMD will acquire 100% of the
outstanding equity of Silo AI for $4.9 billion in cash. The transaction
has been approved by the boards of directors of both companies and is
not subject to a shareholder vote. The deal is expected to close in
the second quarter of 2026, subject to customary regulatory approvals.

"This acquisition marks a significant milestone in AMD's AI strategy,"
said Dr. Lisa Su, Chair and CEO of AMD. "Silo AI's team and technology
will accelerate the deployment of our end-to-end AI solutions."

Silo AI, headquartered in Helsinki, Finland, brings approximately 450
engineers and deep expertise in AI model optimization and deployment.
Post-close, Silo AI will operate as part of AMD's Data Center Solutions
Group.
