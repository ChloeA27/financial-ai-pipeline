Apple Inc. Declares $0.52 Quarterly Dividend

CUPERTINO, CALIFORNIA — April 30, 2026 — Apple Inc. (NASDAQ: AAPL) today
announced that its Board of Directors has declared a cash dividend of $0.52
per share of common stock, representing a 4% increase from the previous
quarterly dividend of $0.50 per share.

The dividend will be paid out on May 21, 2026. Investors who are on the
company's books by the close of business on May 12, 2026, are entitled to
the payout. Please note that the stock will start trading without the right
to this dividend on May 8, 2026.

"With extraordinary confidence in Apple's continued innovation and strong
cash generation, we are pleased to increase our quarterly dividend for the
fourteenth consecutive year," said Tim Cook, Apple's Chief Executive Officer.

This regular cash distribution remains a core component of our capital
return strategy. As of the end of the last fiscal quarter, Apple had
returned over $700 billion to shareholders through dividends and share
repurchases combined. The company continues to maintain a healthy balance
sheet with robust free cash flow generation, supporting both organic
investment and shareholder returns.

For more information regarding dividend reinvestment options and
tax implications, eligible shareholders should consult their financial
advisor or refer to our investor relations website. This press release
contains forward-looking statements regarding the company's capital
allocation plans and is not intended to serve as tax or investment advice.
